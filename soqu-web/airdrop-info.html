Pre-mainnet liquidity token for Soqucoin™ (SOQ™), launching December 6, 2025

What is sSOQ?

sSOQ is a Solana SPL token designed as a "quantum meme incubator" to foster pre-mainnet liquidity, community growth, and educational outreach around post-quantum cryptographic concepts. It serves as a bridge between high-frequency DeFi activity on Solana and Soqucoin's long-term quantum-resistant proof-of-work security model.

Airdrop Eligibility

To be eligible for the sSOQ airdrop, participants must:

  • Be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction)
  • Not be a "U.S. Person" as defined in Regulation S under the U.S. Securities Act of 1933
  • Not be located in any jurisdiction subject to OFAC sanctions or where digital asset offers are restricted
  • Join our community on Discord or X (Twitter)
  • Complete the airdrop registration form (coming soon)

Distribution Mechanism

There is no public token sale. sSOQ distribution occurs through:

  • Community Programs: Discord/X-based engagement rewards
  • Airdrops: Direct distribution to eligible wallet addresses
  • Liquidity Incentives: Rewards for early DeFi participation

Intended 1:1 SOQ Peg

Following Soqucoin mainnet launch (currently targeted for Q1 2026), a bidirectional bridge is intended to enable sSOQ ↔ SOQ transfers on a 1:1 basis.

⚠️ Important: This peg is aspirational only and contingent on:

  • Technical implementation and independent security audits
  • Third-party oracle infrastructure (e.g., Wormhole)
  • Evolving regulatory requirements
No assurance is given that any bridge or peg will be implemented, maintained, or function as described.
